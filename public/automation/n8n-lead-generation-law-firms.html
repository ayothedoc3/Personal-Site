Why this workflow matters

  • Automated lead capture from multiple sources including website forms, social media, and email inquiries

Workflow Overview

The n8n workflow connects your firm's lead capture points to your CRM and communication tools. When a potential client submits their information, the workflow automatically categorizes the lead, assigns it to the appropriate attorney or team member, and triggers relevant follow-up sequences.

Implementation Steps

  2. Create lead capture triggers for various input sources
  3. Design lead qualification rules and scoring criteria
  4. Build automated follow-up sequences and notification systems
  5. Implement lead routing logic based on practice areas
  6. Configure reporting and analytics dashboards
