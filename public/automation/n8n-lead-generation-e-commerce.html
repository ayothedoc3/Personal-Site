Workflow Overview

This n8n workflow automates lead generation for e-commerce by capturing new customer data from your store platform. It then enriches this information and seamlessly adds the new lead to your CRM, initiating personalized follow-up campaigns.

Implementation Steps

  1. Define Lead Sources & Triggers: Identify where your leads originate (e.g., website forms, abandoned carts, ad campaigns) and configure the corresponding n8n trigger node (e.g., Webhook, Shopify, Google Forms).
  2. Extract & Standardize Lead Data: Use n8n nodes to capture relevant lead information (name, email, phone, product interest, cart details) and transform it into a consistent format.
  3. Implement Lead Qualification Logic: Add conditional logic (e.g., IF nodes) to score or segment leads based on criteria like cart value, specific product views, or form responses.
  4. Integrate with CRM/Marketing Tools: Connect n8n to your CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce), email marketing platform (e.g., Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign), or a simple Google Sheet to store and manage the leads.
  5. Automate Follow-up & Nurturing: Design subsequent workflow steps to automatically send personalized welcome emails, add leads to specific nurturing sequences, or create tasks for your sales team.
